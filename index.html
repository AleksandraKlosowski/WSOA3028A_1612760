Why Use Our Services?

JINNE Floristry is a locally founded and locally grown floristry. It also includes cute little nurseries, should you want to add beautiful, lasting flowers throughout your home and garden. supporting local means re-investing into our community and ultimately providing jobs for your fellow friends and community members.

Obviously, we are environmentally friendly. We are passionate about our work and often provide our services with love and passion. We focus on traditionally South African plants, but do expand further into more exotic plants. our main sources of growing include propogation and growing the seeds ourselves which allows a greater investment into the business but does however take more time and dedication.

Our Shop History

It was founded by my nonna Giulia in 1956. She was a traditional womomen from the town of Napoli in Italy, Italy's largest city. She immigrated to South Africa with her father Vincenzo. In Napoli, nonna learnt to cook at a very young age as her father was consistently working and her mother was no longer alive. Napoli was a relatively quiet place so she spent most of her time endulging in her hobbies: gardening. She grew such a passion for flowers, herbs and shrubbery that she spent all of her savings on opening the shop we know today. It was called Nonna's Flowers. Being born here, I felt the need to rename and revamp the shop for a more modern and personalized feel; hence the use of "jinne" in our name.
